How we apply these skills

Alongside commercial services, we operate in macro-areas covering the entire software lifecycle — from industrial automation to artificial intelligence.

Industrial automation, logistics and artificial intelligence

Software and AI agents for managing and optimizing production, logistics and document processes.

  • WMS (Warehouse Management System) — warehouse management: inventory monitoring, shipment planning, storage optimization, integration with logistics systems.
  • MES (Manufacturing Execution System) — monitoring and control of production operations, traceability and real-time visibility of industrial activities.
  • HMI (Human Machine Interface) — intuitive, customized human-machine interfaces for plant control and monitoring.
  • AI agents — automation of document workflows, decision support and non-conformity management with LLM agents.

Systems integration and AI platforms

We connect industrial systems and AI platforms, on-premise and hybrid.

  • Integration between ERP, WMS, MES and HMI systems.
  • Connections to industrial devices: PLCs, robots, IoT sensors.
  • Integration of LLM models and AI agents into existing operational workflows.

Technical support and maintenance

Post-implementation support for industrial software and AI solutions, ensuring optimal performance over time.

  • Technical support and troubleshooting, including remote.
  • Diagnosis and resolution of issues on industrial software and AI agents.
  • Updates and improvements to adapt systems to new operational requirements.

Consulting and training

Process analysis, Industry 4.0 and AI consulting, staff training.

  • Analysis of business processes to identify automation and AI opportunities.
  • Consulting for the adoption of Industry 4.0 technologies and AI solutions.
  • Staff training on software tools, LIRA and AI agents.

Artificial intelligence solutions

In addition to traditional development services, we design and build custom AI solutions: LLM agents, on-premise RAG, process automation — all integrated with your systems, without giving away data to third parties.

AI agents for process automation

We design and develop LLM-based agents that automate complex workflows: from document processing to non-conformity management, to decision support in production.

  • Process analysis and identification of AI automation opportunities
  • Development of LLM agents integrated with your systems (ERP, WMS, MES)
  • Agents with tool use: AI doesn’t just answer, it performs actions on your data
  • On-premise deployment, without sending data to third-party cloud services

LIRA — The AI that knows your documents

Our on-premise RAG product: precise answers built on your documents, not the internet. Perfect for technical manuals, contracts, procedures and regulatory archives.

  • Verifiable answers with precise source citations
  • Indexing of PDFs, docx, images and technical documents
  • Fully on-premise, no data sent outside
  • OpenAI-compatible API and MCP server to integrate into your tools
